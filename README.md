## 📌 Project Overview

This portfolio exists to bridge the gap between creative storytelling and AI engineering — demonstrating that technical depth and creative vision are not opposites but complements.

It is built for **recruiters, technical teams, and creative agencies** who are looking for someone who can operate at the intersection of AI, content, and strategy.

It solves a core problem: showing practical, real-world applications of AI in content workflows, marketing pipelines, and engineering projects — not just listing skills on paper.
